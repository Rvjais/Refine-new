    About Dr. Chirag Kotecha- Dermatologist in Uganda

    Dr.Chirag Kotecha:

    Dr. Chirag Kotecha is a renowned expert in cosmetics and aesthetics, seamlessly blending science and beauty. Graduating with distinction in Medicine from St George University of London, he also holds an MBA from Imperial College, London. With a dual degree in Medicine and Genetics.

    In 2012, he founded Avane Cosmetic Clinique & Medispa in Uganda, introducing innovative techniques such as Electrosurgery and Ablative CO2 Laser Therapy. As the best dermatologist in Kampala, Uganda, Dr. Kotecha ensures exceptional medical services at UMC Victoria Hospital, where he serves as Medical Director.

    As Medical Director of UMC Victoria Hospital, Dr. Kotecha ensures exceptional medical services. He travels to the United States annually for training as a USA Board Certified practitioner in Aesthetics, Pain Management, and Functional Medicine. Driven by compassion and attention to detail, he creates personalized treatment plans that enhance appearances and elevate confidence.

    With Dr. Chirag Kotecha, you can embark on a transformative journey toward beauty, wellness, and self-discovery, as he embraces the unique beauty of each individual. Trust his expertise for a radiant transformation.
